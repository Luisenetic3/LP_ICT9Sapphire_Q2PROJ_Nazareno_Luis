Beef Chow Fun

Ingredients:
  • 140 g beef, preferably flank/skirt steak (5oz)
  • 1 teaspoon light soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Shaoxing rice wine
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cooking oil
  • 450 g sa-ho fun (16oz, see note 1)
  • 2 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 60 g beansprouts (2oz )
  • Â¼ onion, thinly sliced
  • 30 g Chinese chives (1oz, see note 2)
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
Step-by-Step Recipe:
  1. Slice the beef against the grain. Mix with light soy sauce, rice wine, cornstarch, water and sugar. Coat with oil then leave to rest for 15 minutes.
  2. If using fresh ones: Gently separate the sa-ho fun by hand into long strands (try not to break them into small pieces).
  3. If using dried ones: follow the instructions on the package to cook then rinse under cold water prior to stir-frying.
  4. Pour oil into a hot wok. Stir in the marinated beef slices. Fry until they just turn pale (do not overcook). Dish out and leave the oil in the wok.
  5. Put in sa-ho fun. Gently stir around with a pair of chopsticks. When the sa-ho fun begins to wilt, add in beansprouts and onion. Fry for 30 seconds or so.
  6. Add the beef and Chinese chives. Pour in light soy sauce and dark soy sauce. Cook for a further 30 seconds.
